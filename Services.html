OUR SERVICES

Togawa Library provides a wide range of services designed to support learning, research, and community engagement. Our services are delivered by professional librarians and information specialists who are committed to assisting users in accessing, evaluating, and utilizing information effectively.

Through both on-site and digital services, Togawa Library ensures that users receive reliable support in navigating information resources in an increasingly complex knowledge environment.

REFERENCE & RESEARCH SERVICES

Our reference and research services are designed to assist users in identifying relevant information sources, developing research strategies, and accessing authoritative materials. Librarians provide personalized support tailored to individual research needs.

  • Research consultation and reference interviews
  • Assistance with information searching and source evaluation
  • Guidance on the use of catalogs, databases, and digital resources
  • Support for academic, professional, and personal research projects

DIGITAL & ONLINE SERVICES

Togawa Library offers a range of digital services that enable users to access information resources beyond the physical library space. These services support flexible learning and remote research.

  • Remote access to electronic journals and digital collections
  • Online reference assistance and research support
  • Access to digital repositories and institutional publications
  • Technology-assisted learning resources

USER EDUCATION & INFORMATION LITERACY

Togawa Library is committed to enhancing information literacy and critical thinking skills among its users. Educational services are designed to empower users to effectively locate, evaluate, and ethically use information.

  • Library orientation sessions for new users
  • Workshops on research skills and information evaluation
  • Training on digital literacy and responsible information use
  • Guided learning programs in collaboration with educational institutions

COMMUNITY & SUPPORT SERVICES

In addition to information services, Togawa Library actively supports community engagement through programs and services that encourage collaboration, creativity, and lifelong learning.

  • Reading programs and educational events
  • Public lectures, seminars, and workshops
  • Collaborative spaces for group learning and discussion
  • Assistance for users with special accessibility needs
