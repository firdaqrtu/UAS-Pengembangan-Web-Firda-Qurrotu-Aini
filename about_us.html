PROFILE

Togawa Library is a public library established and fully funded by Togawa Corporation as part of its long-term commitment to social responsibility, education, and sustainable knowledge development. The library serves as an open and inclusive learning space designed to support lifelong learning for individuals from diverse backgrounds.

Since its founding, Togawa Library has aimed to bridge information gaps by providing equitable access to reliable resources, both in print and digital formats. The library supports academic research, personal enrichment, and community-based learning through curated collections, professional services, and innovative programs.

Guided by the principles of accessibility, integrity, and innovation, Togawa Library continues to evolve as a knowledge hub that empowers communities and encourages intellectual growth in a rapidly changing information landscape.

VISION & MISSION

Our Vision

To become a trusted public knowledge hub that promotes equitable access to information, supports lifelong learning, and empowers communities through reliable, innovative, and inclusive library services.

Our Mission

  • To provide open and equitable access to diverse, high-quality information resources in both physical and digital formats.
  • To support education, research, and personal development through professional library services and curated collections.
  • To foster information literacy, critical thinking, and responsible knowledge use among library users.
  • To create a welcoming, inclusive, and sustainable library environment for individuals from all backgrounds.
  • To continuously innovate and adapt library services in response to technological advancements and community needs.

FACILITIES

Togawa Library is equipped with modern facilities designed to create a comfortable, inclusive, and productive learning environment. The library building integrates functional design with a welcoming atmosphere to support various learning styles and information needs.

  • Spacious reading areas for individual and group study
  • Reference and research service desks staffed by professional librarians
  • Digital access terminals and high-speed internet connectivity
  • Quiet zones and collaborative discussion rooms
  • Multi-purpose spaces for workshops, lectures, and community programs
  • Accessible facilities to support users with special needs

Through continuous improvement of its facilities, Togawa Library strives to remain a dynamic space where knowledge, creativity, and collaboration thrive.
