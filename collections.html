OUR COLLECTIONS

Togawa Library offers a diverse and continuously expanding range of collections designed to support education, research, and lifelong learning. Our collections are carefully curated to ensure relevance, reliability, and accessibility for users from various academic, professional, and personal backgrounds.

By integrating traditional print resources with digital content, Togawa Library aims to provide comprehensive access to knowledge in multiple formats, responding to the evolving needs of information seekers in the digital era.

PHYSICAL COLLECTIONS

The physical collections at Togawa Library encompass a wide range of printed materials that support academic study, professional development, and general reading interests. These materials are organized and maintained according to established library standards to ensure ease of access and long-term preservation.

  • Books covering science, technology, social sciences, humanities, and business
  • Reference materials such as encyclopedias, dictionaries, and handbooks
  • Printed journals, magazines, and periodicals
  • Reports, policy papers, and institutional publications
  • Special collections related to Togawa Group initiatives and corporate history

Our physical collections are regularly updated to reflect current developments and emerging trends across various fields of knowledge.

DIGITAL COLLECTIONS

Togawa Library’s digital collections provide remote and on-site access to a wide array of electronic resources, enabling users to explore information anytime and anywhere. These collections support flexible learning and research through reliable and user-friendly platforms.

  • E-books and electronic monographs across multiple disciplines
  • Online academic journals and periodical databases
  • Digitized archives and institutional publications
  • Multimedia resources including audio-visual learning materials
  • Research tools and reference databases

Access to digital collections is supported by library staff who provide guidance on effective information searching and responsible use of electronic resources.
